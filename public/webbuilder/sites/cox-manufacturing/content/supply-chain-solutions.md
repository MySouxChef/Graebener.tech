## Screw Machining Supply Chain Solutions

Cox Manufacturing is committed to offering “World-Class” domestic production with technical expertise, reliable quality, and globally competitive pricing – matched with the integrity and reliability of a domestic resource that is every supply-chain manager’s ideal for their precision machined component supplier.

[Request A Quote](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/rfq)

### VOLUME AND CAPACITY

- Cox has over 350 spindles capable of machining complex parts from 4mm to 2.625” in diameter, run by a skilled team of 170+ highly trained employees, scheduled three shifts a day. This guarantees the depth and flexible capacity to support your high-volume production orders are out the door on time and on spec.

### LOGISTICS

- Cox is located in and fully operating out of San Antonio, Texas and has been since it was established in 1956. Cox is a logistically ideal supplier of precision machined components without the long supply chain worries, risks of intellectual property loss, and assorted integrity issues that third world and overseas sourcing can bring.

### ADVANCED QUALITY SYSTEM

- Cox is ISO certified and ITAR registered, commonly handling PPAP and DFARS standards required by the world’s most demanding industries.
- Beyond this, Cox has developed a proprietary patented “Advanced Unity Control” [Quality System](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/quality-control) that leverages Industry 4.0 technology to interpret real-time machine data and quality statistics that takes shop floor quality to an unprecedented level and applies the system to every job.

### LEAN MANUFACTURING

- Cox routinely operates Kanban and other customer-driven quality and inventory management systems, all managed with our proprietary ERP software that integrates functions across the company.

### INVENTORY AND SUPPLY MANAGEMENT

- Being a high-volume production machining and [screw machine products](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/swiss-machining-swiss-turn) supplier, Cox specializes in annual contracts and blanket orders with min-max inventory agreements, as well as fixed firm orders, to consistently supply your parts at optimal costs and delivery at reduced inventory levels.

### VALUE ENGINEERING SUPPORT

- As a domestic supplier with decades of expertise and broad [industry](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/industries) experience supported with an extensive in-house engineering group, Cox commonly assists customers in lowering the “Total Cost of Ownership” through a variety of strategies we are always willing to offer to high-volume buyers. There is no time better than the present to start looking for savings!

Another cost-cutting partnership springs from our geographic position along the productivity pipeline between the US and Mexico. [Cox](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/about-us) is a logistically ideal source for precision machining tasks that are beyond the scope of Mexico suppliers. We can also manage an entire production process in-house, or through partnerships with Mexico affiliates. The result is world-class savings without the logistics worries, freight costs and intellectual property losses third world and overseas sourcing can bring.
