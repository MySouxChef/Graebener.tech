# Resources

## Instant Answers in the Cox Knowledge Base

The Knowledge Base is your access point to information, specifications and research documents gathered by Cox on CNC turning, CNC machining and automatic screw machine manufacturing. For questions on materials, consult the companion Machining Materials Guide.

Can’t find the information for a specific application? Consult the ultimate knowledge source: a Cox Manufacturing customer service representative. [Click here](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/contact-us) for a quick email form or complete contact information.
