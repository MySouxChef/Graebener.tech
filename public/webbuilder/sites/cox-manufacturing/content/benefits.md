# Benefits

## Company Perks

[**Company Perks**](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/#)

[**Professional and Educational Growth**](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/#)

[**Opportunities for Personal Growth**](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/#)

Vacation/Sick Pay

- Accrual of PTO (Paid Time Off) starts on the day of hire. Employees accrue a max of 40 hours of vacation during the first year of service, 80 hours after two years of service, and up to 200 hours (5 weeks) after twenty years of service.


Health Care

- Company-sponsored health insurance is available on the 1st of the month following 60 days of service. Cox employees have access to medical, dental, vision, life, and supplemental insurance.


Paid Holidays

- Full time employees receive five standard paid holidays per year, with additional days added based on performance.


401(K) Profit Sharing/Retirement Plan (Part-time included)

- Cox offers a matched-contribution 401(K) program to all employees, including part-time.


Recruiting Bonus

- Employees that refer a new employee can receive up to a $400 bonus, as well as a catered lunch for the referring employee’s department.


Company Bonus

- Cox pays out a monthly bonus based on the productivity of factory labor and value added.


Fred Pryor Seminars

Cox employees that seek to enhance their professional skills can attend local Fred Pryor seminars at no charge. Department-specific topics such as payroll law and financing are available, as well as crash-courses applicable to everyone, such as leadership, communication, and business writing.

Tooling U-SME: Manufacturing Training Online Access

Our [Apprenticeship Program](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/apprentice) utilizes the free online classes available from Tooling U-SME, but you don’t need to be a machinist to access them. Any employee can review the classes to learn more what makes the industry tick.

Apprentice Program in Partnership with Wayland University and the US Department of Labor

Our [Apprenticeship Program](https://www.coxmanufacturing.com/apprentice) is certified and registered with the Department of Labor. We’ve also partnered with Wayland Baptist University to offer the equivalent of 33 college credit hours towards an industry degree—in most cases, these hours also transfer to credit at other institutions, too.

College Tuition Reimbursement

We currently offer 100% reimbursement for college tuition in books for classes relevant to an approved degree in your field, no matter what position you hold.

RightNow Media Library Access

Every employee (and their family) has access to thousands of professional resources on the RightNow Media online library and app, including company favorites such as “The Ideal Team Player”, by Patrick Lencioni.

Opportunities to attend industry trade shows and conferences

The Cox leadership team frequents many major industry conferences and trade shows annually. Check out our Events (link to “Events” page) page to see where we’re going next.

Wellness Program with Monthly Education

We care about our employees’ health—physical, mental, and financial. Every month, employees attend a meeting addressing common health issues. Employees also get the chance to participate in employee-led contests throughout the year.

Dave Ramsey’s Financial Peace University Sponsorship

All new employees are encouraged to attend Dave Ramsey’s Financial Peace University, a 10-lesson weekly course completely paid for by Cox, to learn how to never worry about money again. Sessions are held locally in a small group setting for community, motivation, and accountability.

Weekly Life Learner Luncheons

Cox employees have access to attend optional weekly lunch meetings to discuss personal and spiritual subjects and foster a sense of community within the company. Meetings are on designated days for male and female employees to comfortably address more personal issues.

RightNow Media Library Access

Every employee (and their family) has access to thousands of resources on the RightNow Media online library and app, including family-friendly favorites like “VeggieTales”, as well as valuable content for personal care, focusing on subjects such as marriage, parenting, finances, and mental health.

River City Believers Academy PreK-12 Tuition Reimbursement

Cox offers an 80% contribution towards tuition for employees interested in having their children attend a fully accredited local private Christian school with small classes in a wholesome environment.

Weekend to Remember Sponsorships

Weekend to Remember is a two-and-a-half-day getaway for couples to invest in and strengthen the foundation of their relationship in a distraction-free, biblically-centered setting. Cox provides a special hotel and registration discount for employees that are new or returning guests.

Access to Marketplace Chaplains

Cox has partnered with Marketplace Chaplains to provide the employees with confidants that build relationships of trust and friendship. Both male and female chaplains are available on-site weekly to discuss personal and spiritual issues and extend support for the emotional well-being of employees and their family members.
