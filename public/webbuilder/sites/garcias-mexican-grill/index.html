Nuestra Historia

Family Recipes, San Antonio Soul

At Garcia's, every dish tells a story. Our recipes have been passed down through generations — from our abuela's kitchen to your table. We believe the best Mexican food starts with the freshest ingredients, prepared with care, and served with warmth.

Open early for the breakfast crowd and serving through lunch, we're the neighborhood spot where regulars become family. Whether it's your first visit or your hundredth, you'll always leave with a full stomach and a smile.
