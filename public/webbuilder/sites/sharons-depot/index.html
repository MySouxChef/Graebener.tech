The story

From a 53-foot container in the garage — to your trusted San Antonio depot.

In 2016, Sharon was left without a job. This time, she would not allow Corporate America to dictate her future and decided to start her own venture. Through an old friend, she got in touch with Home Depot and bought her first truckload of merchandise. The following week, a 53-foot container was unloaded in her garage. Within days, most of it had sold.

A few months later — short on space and with a few upset neighbors — we moved into our current warehouse on Stahl Park. We've met many wonderful people on this journey, and we're grateful to have built a business that works for everyone.

"We treat every customer like family, and many of our shoppers have been with us for years. Loyal customers always receive the best deals, because your trust means everything to us." — Sharon

Whether you're a contractor stocking up for the week, a homeowner finishing a weekend project, or just hunting for a real deal — Sharon's Depot is where San Antonio shops for brand-new tools at brand-new prices, minus a chunk.
