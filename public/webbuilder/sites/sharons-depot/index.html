The story

From the back of a pickup — to a south-side surplus shop you can count on.

In 2012, Frank came home from a tour with the Texas Air National Guard to find his old job gone. Through a buddy in freight, he scraped together enough to buy a single pallet of overstock from a Home Depot return center — a beat-up truckload of tools, shelving, and a couple of busted-up shop vacs. He and his daughter sold most of it out of the back of his pickup the following weekend.

A few months later — short on space and one HOA letter to the wise — we moved into a leased bay on Bandera Rd. We've been there ever since. Same family on the floor. Same handshake price for our regulars.

"We don't dress our prices up and we don't dress our tools down. Real surplus, fair prices, every time. Our regulars get the regulars' price — that's how it's been since day one." — Frank

Whether you're a contractor stocking up for the week, a homeowner finishing a weekend project, or just hunting for a real deal — Lone Star Surplus is where San Antonio shops for brand-new tools at brand-new prices, minus a chunk.
