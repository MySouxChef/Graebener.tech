## About (homepage copy, lightly edited)

At Sharon's Depot, we take pride in being a family-owned business that has been serving our community for over 10 years. We specialize in offering new, high-quality Home Depot items and tools at discounted prices — most of our products come from overstock inventory, so you get brand-new merchandise for less.

Our goal is to provide great value without compromising on quality. We treat every customer like family, and many of our shoppers have been with us for years. At Sharon's Depot, loyal customers always receive the best deals, because your trust and satisfaction means everything to us.

Whether you're a professional, or just looking for reliable tools at affordable prices, Sharon's Depot is your trusted destination for quality and savings.

## Origin story (from /about)

In 2016, Sharon was left without a job. This time, she would not allow Corporate America to dictate her future and decided to start her own venture. After many sleepless nights, she decided to take a leap of faith; through an old friend, Sharon got in touch with Home Depot and purchased her first truckload of merchandise. The following week, a 53' container was unloaded in our garage. Within days, most of the merchandise had been sold! A few months later, our garage stopped being a viable option (not only lack of space, but several upset neighbors 😀) and we moved to our current warehouse.

We have met many wonderful people during this journey. We are happy to have started a business that benefits every party involved. We are a family owned and operated company and we will continue to provide great merchandise at wonderful prices. Thank you very much for your support!
