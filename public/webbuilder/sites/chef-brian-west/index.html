About

Chef Brian West

A native Texan who exploded onto San Antonio's culinary scene with Café Paladar and never looked back. From serving as operational chef on Food Network's Restaurant Impossible (21 episodes) to teaching at the Culinary Institute of America, Brian has spent two decades defining what Texas cuisine means.

He created the Texas Culinary Manifesto, co-founded the Texas Culinary Alliance, and serves as the official chef of the San Antonio Stock Show & Rodeo. His West Wednesday segments air weekly on Fox 29.
